### Jerry Dipoto's Strategy
- **Pitching depth** - Always stockpiling arms
- **Defensive versatility** - Multi-position players valued
- **Contact over power** - Though 2026 roster shifts toward power
- **Player development** - Patience with prospects, internal promotions
- **Aggressive trading** - Always looking for marginal improvements

### Scott Servais' Management Style  
- **Matchup-heavy** - Uses numbers and platoon advantages
- **Communication-first** - Players know their roles
- **Development-focused** - Especially with young pitchers
- **Defensive positioning** - Heavily analytical approach
